Other curricula

This curriculum is one of a few efforts to inform learners how they can pursue Heritage Studies independently. Below are a few other such efforts.

  • Cultural Heritage Science Open Source (CHSOS) Training Resources – Practical, open-source focused training in heritage science and technical examination.
  • Arches Open Source Heritage Inventory Platform Resources – Guides and learning materials for digital heritage management using the open-source Arches platform.
  • UNESCO Dive into Intangible Cultural Heritage Platform – Interactive explorations and resources on intangible heritage (supplements the MOOC).
